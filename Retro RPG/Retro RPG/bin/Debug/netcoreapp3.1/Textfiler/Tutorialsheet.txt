How to play

In every room you will be greeted by a up to three choices.
to select a choice type in the number that is next to it.

Combat

When in combat you have five options

Slice: Deals the most damage.
Stab: Deals damage through armor.
Shield: Gives you more armor.
Dodge attack: Gives less armor and a chance at taking another turn.
Shred: Destroys some of the enemys armor.
